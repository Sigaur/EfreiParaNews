Prices:


Skydiving may be a little (a lot) expensive. But, thanks to the voluntary way of life of the ASPU, jumping with them is the cheapest way to do it. Yes, you heard alright, one of the best formation in France also happens to be the cheapest.



Annual costs:


ASPU Membership Fees 80€
PUC Membership Fees (only the first year) 50€
FFP Licence and insurance 135€
Dropzone Membership Fees 20€
TOTAL 285€




Jumping Fees:


4 000m Jump 27€
Housing 10€/day
Camping 2€/day
Pension 7.50€
